A Personal Transformation at the Heart of Learning

At first, I thought this adventure would be easy. I was convinced that my personal motivation would be enough to overcome all obstacles. However, I quickly realized that this wasn’t the case. While motivation is essential, it alone couldn’t carry me through my journey. What truly allowed me to progress was the method of positive feedback, both in receiving and offering constructive comments. Thanks to this approach, I was able to improve and achieve milestones I never thought possible.

The Importance of Human Skills

It became clear to me that my motivation, although important, was not sufficient. I needed something more concrete to help me navigate the complexities of human relationships. This "something" was the knowledge of human skills. These skills encompass a variety of methods and techniques we learned during our foundational training and bootcamp. They include effective communication, active listening, empathy, and collaboration. They are essential in today’s world, where social interaction is at the core of almost all professional and personal successes.

A Profound Impact

By deepening my knowledge in this area, I discovered the importance of personal and collective well-being. I learned to live better in society, to interact constructively with others, and these new skills had a direct impact on my family life. I now feel more in tune with my family members, able to establish open and sincere dialogues, which has strengthened our bonds. Communication, once tamed, has become a valuable tool for easing tensions and fostering a positive atmosphere at home.

Preparing for the Next Step

Today, I feel ready to tackle the next stage of my journey: beginning my training in cybersecurity. This field deeply interests me, not only for its technical challenges but also for the importance of digital security in our current society. The transition to this new domain wouldn’t be possible without the foundation of human skills I have acquired. I now understand that cybersecurity is not just about technology; it also requires the ability to work in teams, communicate clearly, and solve problems collaboratively.

Recognitions and Thanks

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to Josh Bruse, who had the idea that I could join Dev Academy. This opportunity has changed my life. I also thank all my facilitators, whose commitment and support have allowed me to grow. Every session, every exercise, and every moment of sharing contributed to my personal and professional development. My colleagues from Mako-Kotare-2024 also played a crucial role in this journey. Their presence, encouragement, and exchanges enriched me, pushing me to give my best.

A Vision for the Future

Looking ahead, I am driven by a new vision. I aspire to use the skills I have acquired not only to succeed in the field of cybersecurity but also to continue growing as an individual. I wish to remain engaged in developing my human skills, as I firmly believe that they are key to building strong relationships and a harmonious work environment.

Conclusion

In summary, my journey has been a true revelation. I’ve learned that motivation is a driving force, but human skills are the necessary fuel to move forward. By integrating these elements into my life, I now feel capable of facing new challenges with confidence. My experience at Dev Academy has been more than just technical learning; it has been a springboard for personal transformation. I am now ready to embrace the future, fortified by my knowledge and driven by a desire to learn even more. A new chapter is beginning, and I can’t wait to see where it leads me.
