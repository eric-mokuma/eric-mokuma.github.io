Te Whare Tapa Whā
Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) I usually run three times a week and every Wednesday my sons and I go out to play football
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) To strengthen the family link, every Thursday at 7 p.m., we gather at the house of one of the extended family members for an exchange, lunch, and cup of tea or coffee.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) We (my wife and I) enjoy a moment of encouragement as we reflect on our journey from present to future. We believe this benefits us and encourages us to continue.
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) We (my family and I) believe in a strong God, therefore we have a moment of awe or thanksgiving to Him, and every Monday we have a Bible study group where we spend the evening studying and praying.
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) we (my friends and their families and my family and I) organize a forest hike once every 3 months.
