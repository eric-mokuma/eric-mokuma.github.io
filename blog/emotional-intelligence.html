EQ (Emotional Intelligence)

The EQ (Emotional Intelligence) is the ability of an individual to perceive, comprehend, master, and express one's own emotion, as well as to distinguish and generate an emotion in another.


Abilities:

  • Empathy
  • Emotion control
  • Social skills
  • Self-awareness
  • Conflict resolution

EQ is associated to the interpersonal relationships, leadership, teamwork It is especially significant because it promotes more harmonious interpersonal relationships by improving understanding of others' feelings and needs.
