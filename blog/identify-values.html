  • Impact of Transportation on Learning Journey:

    • Transportation poses a significant challenge that could impact my learning journey during the bootcamp. The complex and time-consuming commute, involving multiple modes of transportation, will require careful planning and flexibility to ensure timely arrival at the bootcamp venue.

    The early morning drop-off of my two-year-old child adds an additional layer of stress and potential delays. The unpredictable nature of childcare and traffic conditions could disrupt my schedule and affect my ability to focus during the first hours of class. Moreover, the extended commute time limits my opportunities for extra study or networking outside of bootcamp hours.


  • Conflict Amongst Colleagues: What Have I Done?

    • As an administrative assistant at a United Nations under-traitance company over a decade ago, I encountered a dilemma involving a conflict of interest. Upon joining the FUEL UNIT department, I observed that the cleaner, who happened to be my superior's girlfriend, was underperforming in her duties. Driven by a desire to improve the efficiency of our workplace, I sought to understand the underlying reasons behind her lackluster performance.

    However, my inquiry was met with resistance, as my superior's personal relationship with the cleaner created a barrier to objective evaluation. Despite my attempts to address the issue professionally, the conflict escalated, creating an uncomfortable and strained work environment. The situation forced me to question my actions and the impact they had on my colleagues and the overall workplace dynamics.

    Upon discovering that the woman was romantically involved with the chef, I felt a surge of trepidation for my own position within the company. In the Democratic Republic of Congo, securing employment is an arduous task, and the prospect of losing my job filled me with anxiety. Given the intimate nature of our workplace, I recognized the need to devise a swift and discreet solution to address the situation.

    Despite my initial attempts to encourage the woman to uphold her professional obligations, my efforts proved futile. Recognizing the potential conflict of interest and the implications it could have on the workplace dynamics, I discreetly reached out to the human resources department. I respectfully requested that she be transferred to a different department, believing that this would mitigate any potential biases or favoritism that could arise from her personal relationship with the chef.
